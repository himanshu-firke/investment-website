Stock Trading

Stock trading broadly refers to any buying and selling of stock, but is colloquially used to refer to more shorter-term investments made by very active investors. Stock trading is a difficult and risky enterprise, but with education, you can work to lower risks and increase your likelihood of success.

Demat Account

A Demat account, or Dematerialized Account, is a digital vault for storing stock investments. Demat accounts are used to buy, sell, and hold financial securities like stocks, bonds, and mutual funds.

Mutual Funds

A mutual fund is a collective investment vehicle that collects & pools money from a number of investors and invests the same in equities, bonds, government securities, money market instruments.

SIP

SIP stands for Systematic Investment Plan. SIP is a method of investing in mutual funds where investors regularly invest a fixed amount of money at predetermined intervals (such as monthly or quarterly) over a specified period.
